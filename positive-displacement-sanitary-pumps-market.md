<p><ul><li>Rotary Positive Displacement Sanitary Pumps</li><li>Reciprocating Positive Displacement Sanitary Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Positive displacement sanitary pumps are categorized into two main types: rotary and reciprocating. Rotary positive displacement pumps use rotating elements, like gears or lobes, to move fluid, ideal for handling thick or viscous materials. These pumps ensure consistent flow and are widely used in food and beverage applications. In contrast, reciprocating positive displacement pumps utilize a back-and-forth motion to transport fluids, offering precise control over flow rate and pressure, making them suitable for applications requiring high accuracy and sanitation standards.</p></p>
