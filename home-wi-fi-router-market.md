<p><p>The home Wi-Fi router market is primarily divided into fixed Wi-Fi routers and mobile Wi-Fi routers. Fixed Wi-Fi routers are designed for stationary use within a home, providing stable, high-speed internet to multiple devices through a wired or wireless connection. In contrast, mobile Wi-Fi routers are portable devices that allow users to connect to the internet on-the-go, often using cellular networks. Both types serve distinct needs, catering to either stationary home environments or dynamic, mobile lifestyles.</p></p>
