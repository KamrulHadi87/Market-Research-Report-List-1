<p><strong>The Projector for Volumetric Display Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Digital Light Processing (DLP) Technology</li><li>Liquid Crystal on Silicon (LCOS) Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The projector for volumetric display market is segmented primarily into two technologies: Digital Light Processing (DLP) and Liquid Crystal on Silicon (LCOS). DLP technology utilizes micro-mirrors to reflect light and create images, offering high brightness and fast response times, making it ideal for dynamic content. In contrast, LCOS technology employs liquid crystals on silicon panels, producing high-resolution images with rich colors and deep blacks. Both technologies cater to various applications, including entertainment, education, and medical visualization, enhancing the volumetric display experience.</p></p>
