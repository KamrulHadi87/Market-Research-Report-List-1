<p><p>The Continuous Positive Airway Pressure (CPAP) system market comprises two primary types: Automatic CPAP (APAP) and Non-Automatic CPAP. Automatic CPAP devices adjust airflow pressure automatically based on the user's breathing patterns, providing optimal support throughout the night. In contrast, Non-Automatic CPAP devices deliver a fixed pressure, requiring users to set the pressure manually. Both types are crucial for managing sleep apnea, with preferences varying based on individual comfort, needs, and medical recommendations, influencing market dynamics and growth.</p></p>
