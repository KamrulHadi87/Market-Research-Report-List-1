<p><p>Real-Time Location Systems (RTLS) are technologies that enable the tracking and management of assets or personnel in real time. Key market types include:</p><p>1. **RFID**: Utilizes radio waves for short-range identification and tracking.</p><p>2. **Wi-Fi**: Leverages existing wireless networks for location tracking.</p><p>3. **Ultrasound**: Uses sound waves to determine positions with high accuracy.</p><p>4. **Infrared**: Employs light beams for proximity detection.</p><p>5. **Zigbee**: A low-power, mesh networking standard ideal for IoT applications.</p><p>6. **Ultra Wide Band (UWB)**: Delivers precise location data using a wide frequency spectrum.</p></p>
