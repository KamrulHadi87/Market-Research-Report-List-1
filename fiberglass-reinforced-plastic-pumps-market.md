<p><p>The Fiberglass Reinforced Plastic (FRP) Pumps market is categorized into horizontal and vertical pumps, each designed for specific applications. Horizontal FRP pumps are typically used for transferring fluids across long distances and are ideal for processes requiring high flow rates. In contrast, vertical FRP pumps are designed for limited space applications and are ideal for handling fluids from below ground, such as in sump or tank operations. Both types leverage the corrosion resistance and durability of fiberglass reinforced plastic for enhanced performance.</p></p>
