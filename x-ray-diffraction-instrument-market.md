<p><p>The X-Ray Diffraction (XRD) instrument market is categorized into two main types: Powder XRD and Single-Crystal XRD. Powder XRD is primarily used for analyzing crystalline materials in powdered form, providing valuable information about phase identification and crystallographic structure. In contrast, Single-Crystal XRD is utilized for detailed analysis of individual crystal structures, offering precise data on atomic arrangements. Both types play crucial roles in materials science, chemistry, and solid-state physics by facilitating understanding of material properties and behaviors.</p></p>
