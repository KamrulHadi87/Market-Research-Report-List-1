<p><p>The Micro-Magnetic Resonance Imaging (Micro-MRI) market features several types of MRI systems catering to different imaging needs. Open MRI offers a spacious design, reducing patient anxiety but may have limitations in resolution. Closed MRI provides higher image quality in a confined space, while Standard Bore MRI has a conventional tube design. Wide-Bore MRI bridges the gap, offering a larger opening for patient comfort without compromising image quality, making it suitable for larger individuals and claustrophobic patients.</p></p>
