HD Cameras provide higher resolution images, enhancing clarity and detail while still utilizing coaxial cables. IP Cameras, on the other hand, operate over internet protocols, enabling high-definition video and remote access, making them suitable for modern, scalable surveillance systems with advanced features like motion detection and cloud storage.</p></p>
