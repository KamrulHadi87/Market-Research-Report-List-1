<p><p>The Baby Clinical Nutrition Market encompasses three primary types of administration: </p><p>1. **Oral Administration** involves feeding infants specialized formulas or supplements directly, beneficial for those who can feed normally. </p><p>2. **Enteral Administration** uses feeding tubes for infants unable to consume food orally, ensuring adequate nutrient delivery. </p><p>3. **Intravenous Administration** delivers nutrients directly into the bloodstream for critically ill infants who cannot digest food, providing essential vitamins and minerals for optimal growth and development. Each method addresses specific nutritional needs based on the infant's health status.</p></p>
