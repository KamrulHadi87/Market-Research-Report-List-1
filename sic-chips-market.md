<p><p>The SiC chips market is primarily divided into two types: SiC trench gate chips and SiC planar gate chips. SiC trench gate chips feature a vertical structure that enhances efficiency and thermal performance, making them suitable for high-power applications. In contrast, SiC planar gate chips utilize a traditional horizontal design, offering ease of manufacturing and integration. Both types are essential in various sectors, including automotive, renewable energy, and industrial applications, driving the adoption of SiC technology for improved performance and energy efficiency.</p></p>
