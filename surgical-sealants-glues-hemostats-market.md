<p><p>The surgical sealants, glues, and hemostats market comprises three primary types: sealants, medical glues, and hemostats. Medical glues serve as adhesives to bond tissues together for surgical repairs, often with rapid-setting capabilities. Hemostats are agents that control bleeding by promoting coagulation or acting as physical barriers. Together, these products enhance surgical outcomes and reduce complications, making them essential in various medical procedures.</p></p>
