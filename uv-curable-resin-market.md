<p><p>The UV-curable resin market is segmented into two main types: non-acrylates and acrylates. Non-acrylates, including oligomers and oligoamines, are known for their low shrinkage and excellent adhesion properties, making them suitable for applications in coatings and adhesives. In contrast, acrylates are widely used due to their versatility, rapid curing, and diverse formulation options, often utilized in printing inks, coatings, and sealants. Both types contribute significantly to advancements in sustainable and efficient curing technologies across various industries.</p></p>
