<p><p>The sterile diaphragm valve market is segmented into four main types: manual, pneumatic, electric, and other. Manual valves require human operation for controlling flow, providing simplicity and reliability. Pneumatic valves use compressed air to actuate the diaphragm, offering quick response times and automation in process control. Electric valves rely on electric actuators, allowing for precise control and integration with modern automation systems. Other types may include specialized configurations or hybrid systems, catering to specific industry needs.</p></p>
