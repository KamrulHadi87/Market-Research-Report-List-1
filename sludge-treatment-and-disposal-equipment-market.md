<p><p>Sludge treatment and disposal equipment includes various technologies for managing wastewater sludge. Screw presses dewater sludge by using a rotating mechanism to squeeze out water. Centrifuges use high-speed rotation to separate solids from liquids, enhancing the dewatering process. Belt filter presses employ a continuous belt system to separate water from sludge through pressure and gravity. Other equipment may include rotary drum thickeners and static screens, each tailored for specific sludge characteristics and treatment needs, ensuring effective waste management.</p></p>
