<p><strong>The GaN FET Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Depletion Mode</li><li>Enhancement Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The GaN FET market is primarily categorized into depletion mode and enhancement mode devices. Depletion mode GaN FETs are normally on, requiring a negative gate voltage to turn off, which is beneficial for specific applications needing low power consumption and efficient switching. Conversely, enhancement mode GaN FETs are normally off, requiring a positive gate voltage to operate, making them ideal for high-speed and high-efficiency applications in power electronics. Both types serve distinct roles in advancing technology across various sectors.</p></p>
