<p><p>The Pharmaceutical Rapid Microbiology Testing market encompasses various methodologies for detecting microbial contamination quickly and accurately. Immunology-based methods utilize antibodies for specific pathogen identification. Nucleic acid-based amplification tests target DNA or RNA, enhancing sensitivity and specificity. Mass spectrometry-based methods analyze microbial metabolites or proteins for rapid identification. Flow cytometry assesses physical and chemical characteristics of particles in a fluid. Growth-based detection technologies monitor microbial growth in culture, while biochemical tests evaluate metabolic activity, facilitating efficient quality control in pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
