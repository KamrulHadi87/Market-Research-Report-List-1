<p><p>The melanoma cancer diagnostics market comprises various testing methods, including Fluorescent In Situ Hybridization (FISH) tests, Comparative Genomic Hybridization (CGH) tests, and Immunohistochemical (IHC) tests. FISH tests detect specific genetic abnormalities using fluorescent probes, while CGH tests analyze chromosomal imbalances in tumor samples to reveal genetic alterations. IHC tests identify protein expression patterns in tissue samples, aiding in the differentiation of melanoma from other skin lesions. Each method plays a crucial role in accurate diagnosis and targeted treatment strategies.</p></p>
