<p><p>The UV-LED market is segmented into three main types: UV-A, UV-B, and UV-C LEDs. UV-A LEDs emit longer wavelengths, primarily used in curing applications and certain medical therapies. UV-B LEDs, emitting mid-range wavelengths, are mainly utilized in horticultural lighting and dermatological treatments. UV-C LEDs, with shorter wavelengths, are renowned for their germicidal properties, effectively used in purification and disinfection technologies. Each type caters to distinct applications, driving growth in various industries, including healthcare, agriculture, and environmental safety.</p></p>
