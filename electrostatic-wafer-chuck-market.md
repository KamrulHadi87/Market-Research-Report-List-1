<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Electrostatic Wafer Chuck Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coulomb Type</li><li>Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrostatic wafer chuck market features two primary types: Coulomb Type and Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type. Coulomb Type chucks utilize electrostatic forces generated by charged plates to hold wafers securely in place, providing stability during processing. In contrast, JR Type chucks leverage the Johnsen-Rahbek effect, enhancing holding forces through an electric field between the chuck and wafer, minimizing thermal drifting. Both types are essential in semiconductor manufacturing, offering precise alignment and efficient processing capabilities.</p></p>
