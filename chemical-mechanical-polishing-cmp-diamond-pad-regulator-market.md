<p><p>The Chemical Mechanical Polishing (CMP) diamond pad regulator market comprises several types, including Plated, Brazed, Sintered, and CVD types. Plated regulators feature diamonds adhered to a substrate, offering cost-effectiveness for moderate polishing tasks. Brazed types involve a more robust bond between diamonds and the pad, ideal for demanding applications. Sintered types utilize a heat and pressure process to combine diamonds and metal, providing durability. CVD types are created through chemical vapor deposition, offering precision with advanced diamond structures for high-performance polishing.</p></p>
