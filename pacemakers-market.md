<p><strong>The Pacemakers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Implantable Cardiac Pacemakers</li><li>External Cardiac Pacemakers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pacemaker market can be categorized into two main types: implantable cardiac pacemakers and external cardiac pacemakers. Implantable cardiac pacemakers are surgically placed inside the body, providing long-term management of heart rhythm disorders, while external cardiac pacemakers are used temporarily to regulate heartbeats and are typically utilized in emergency situations or during surgery. Both types are essential for treating arrhythmias, ensuring proper heart function, and enhancing patient quality of life, but they differ in usage, duration, and method of operation.</p></p>
