Cloud Emulsion, on the other hand, incorporates emulsified ingredients that create a visually appealing haze when mixed with alcohol, enriching the drink's aesthetic and texture. Both types serve to elevate alcoholic products, offering unique sensory experiences for consumers.</p></p>
