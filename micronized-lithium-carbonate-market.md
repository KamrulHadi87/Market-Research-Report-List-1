<p><p>The Micronized Lithium Carbonate market is segmented into two main types: Battery Grade and Technical Grade. Battery Grade lithium carbonate is highly purified for use in lithium-ion batteries, ensuring optimal performance and energy efficiency in electric vehicles and portable electronics. In contrast, Technical Grade lithium carbonate is utilized for industrial applications, such as lubricants and glass production, where stringent purity requirements are less critical. Both grades cater to specific needs but share the common base of lithium carbonate.</p></p>
