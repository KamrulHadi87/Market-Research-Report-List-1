<p><ul><li>IntumescentCableCoatings</li><li>Non-IntumescentCableCoatings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial cable coating market is primarily divided into two types: intumescent and non-intumescent cable coatings. Intumescent cable coatings expand and form an insulating char when exposed to heat, providing effective fire resistance and protecting cables from damage during a fire. Non-intumescent cable coatings, on the other hand, create a protective layer without significant expansion, typically offering thermal protection and weather resistance. Both types serve critical roles in enhancing the safety and durability of industrial electrical installations.</p></p>
