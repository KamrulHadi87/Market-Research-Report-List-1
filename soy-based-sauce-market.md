<p><p>The soy-based sauce market comprises primarily two types: brewed soy-based sauce and blended soy-based sauce. Brewed soy-based sauce is traditionally made through a fermentation process using soybeans, wheat, water, and salt, resulting in a rich umami flavor. In contrast, blended soy-based sauce combines brewed soy sauce with additional ingredients, such as flavor enhancers or other sauces, to create a unique taste profile and cater to diverse culinary applications. Both types are popular in global cuisine for enhancing flavor.</p></p>
