<p><ul><li>Linear Scales</li><li>Partial Arc Scales</li><li>Rotary Scales</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical encoders scale market consists of three main types: linear scales, partial arc scales, and rotary scales. Linear scales are used for precise position measurement along a straight line, ideal for applications requiring accurate linear motion tracking. Partial arc scales, designed for limited angular measurements, serve specialized applications where full rotation is unnecessary. Rotary scales encompass full 360-degree measurement capabilities, making them suitable for various rotation-based applications in industries like automation and robotics, ensuring high precision and reliability.</p></p>
