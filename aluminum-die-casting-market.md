<p><p>The aluminum die casting market is segmented into three main types: low pressure die casting, high pressure die casting, and other methods. Low pressure die casting involves the controlled pouring of aluminum to achieve complex shapes with minimal defects, ideal for larger parts. High pressure die casting uses high-speed injection of molten aluminum into molds for precision and fine details, suitable for high-volume production. Other methods may include gravity and sand casting, catering to specific applications and varying production needs.</p></p>
