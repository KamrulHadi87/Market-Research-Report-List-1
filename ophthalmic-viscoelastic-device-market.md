<p><p>The ophthalmic viscoelastic device (OVD) market is primarily segmented into cohesive and dispersive OVDs. Cohesive OVDs offer higher viscosity and are designed to maintain their shape and protect corneal tissues during surgery, making them ideal for anterior segment procedures. Dispersive OVDs, on the other hand, are formulated to spread easily, providing excellent cell protection and maintaining space in the eye. Both types serve essential roles in cataract surgery and other ophthalmic procedures, enhancing surgical outcomes and patient safety.</p></p>
