<p><p>Supervisory circuits play a crucial role in monitoring system parameters and ensuring operational integrity. The market can be segmented into SMD (Surface Mount Device) and Through Hole types. SMD types are favored for compact applications, offering minimal size and weight while enabling high-density packaging on circuit boards. Both types cater to diverse industries, including automotive, telecommunications, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
