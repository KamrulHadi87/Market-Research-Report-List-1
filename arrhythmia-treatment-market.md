<p><strong>The Arrhythmia Treatment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Medication</li><li>Cardioversion</li><li>Catheter Ablation</li><li>Pacemaker</li><li>ICD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The arrhythmia treatment market encompasses several key types. Medication includes antiarrhythmic drugs designed to restore normal heart rhythm. Cardioversion is a procedure that delivers electrical shocks to reset the heart's rhythm. Catheter ablation involves using heat or cold to destroy problematic tissue causing arrhythmias. Pacemakers are devices implanted to regulate heartbeats, while implantable cardioverter-defibrillators (ICD) monitor heart activity and deliver shocks to correct life-threatening arrhythmias, enhancing overall patient care and management of heart rhythm disorders.</p></p>
