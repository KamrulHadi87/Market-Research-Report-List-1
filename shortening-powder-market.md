<p><p>The shortening powder market is primarily categorized into three types: palm shortening powder, soybean shortening powder, and others. Soybean shortening powder, extracted from soybeans, offers a plant-based option with a lower saturated fat content, appealing to health-conscious consumers. The "others" category encompasses various alternatives, including coconut and canola shortening powders, catering to diverse dietary preferences.</p></p>
