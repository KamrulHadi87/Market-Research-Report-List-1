<p><ul><li>Single Glass</li><li>Double Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Refrigerated Display Cases (RDC) glasses come in two main types: single glass and double glass. Single glass showcases use a single pane, providing excellent visibility but less insulation, making them suitable for low-temperature environments. In contrast, double glass options utilize two panes with an air gap, improving thermal efficiency and reducing energy consumption while maintaining product visibility. The choice between these types depends on the required insulation efficiency, energy savings, and the specific needs of the retail environment.</p></p>
