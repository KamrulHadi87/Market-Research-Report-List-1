<p><ul><li>Organic Coating</li><li>Vacuum Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The OLED coating equipment market is primarily categorized into organic coating and vacuum coating types. Organic coating involves applying luminescent materials directly onto substrates to create vibrant displays, focusing on flexibility and efficiency in production. In contrast, vacuum coating employs deposition techniques in a controlled environment to create thin films, ensuring high precision and uniformity. Both methods are essential for developing high-quality OLED displays, catering to diverse applications in consumer electronics, automotive displays, and lighting solutions.</p></p>
