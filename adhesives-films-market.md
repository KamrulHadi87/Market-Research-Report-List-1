<p><p>The Adhesive Films Market comprises several types, each catering to specific applications. Pressure-sensitive adhesives bond on contact and are widely used in labels and tapes. Hot-melt adhesives are thermoplastics that provide strong, quick bonding upon cooling, commonly used in packaging and assembly. Light-cured adhesives require UV light for curing and are ideal for precise applications in electronics and medical devices. Additionally, other adhesives include solvent-based and water-based options, each suited for distinct industrial uses, enhancing versatility in various sectors.</p></p>
